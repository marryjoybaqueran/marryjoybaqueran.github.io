Tiny Tasters

Tiny Tasters is a software-powered mobile application that allows customers to browse and view various types of cakes with its description, as well as doing direct booking through filling out an online “book form”. This app has the followings features and interfaces:

  • You can register in the app with the following information (name, address, e- mail, username, and password), if you don’t have an existing account.
  • You can then log in with your registered username and password.
  • You can choose two main types of cake: personalized and regular cake.
  • You can browse multiple categories under personalized cakes (disney characters, superheroes, flowers and butterflies, games, barbie, and cartoons). While regular cakes contain fourteen basic designs.
  • You can search any items according to your preferences in the search bar.
  • You can rate each cake after reviewing/receiving the product.
  • You can look over the description of the available prices, flavors, sizes, colors, toppers, and shape of each cake.
  • You can book your orders online through filling out the form with your personal information, writing dedication, selecting time and date to deliver, and choosing delivery option (pick-up or door-to-door).
  • You can view the detailed description of the shop, such as location, social media account, and contact information.
  • You can view your listings of orders.
  • You can customize and modify your profile information.
